ABOUT THE
SUMMIT

Across a range of committees, delegates represent assigned countries, examine contemporary international issues, build alliances, draft resolutions and work towards collective outcomes under formal Rules of Procedure.

The summit is designed to combine strong academic preparation with a professional conference experience. Guided by Vox. Visio. Veritas., SORA places emphasis on informed expression, clear perspective and integrity in debate.

Founder, SORA Youth Summit
